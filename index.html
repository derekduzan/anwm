About Us

The ΣΛΒ Alumni Network of West Michigan was established in May of 2014 as the first official Alumni Network in our fraternity's East Region, and one of only a handful in the nation at the time. Our purpose was to unite and support our community, while promoting our organization's values.

To inquire about membership, please request more information from a member of our board in person.

To learn more about our fraternity, visit sigmalambdabeta.com.
