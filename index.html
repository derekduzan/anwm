About Us

The West Michigan Alumni Network of Sigma Lambda Beta International Fraternity, Inc. was established in 2014 to unite and serve our brotherhood in the West Michigan region. As proud members of a Latino-based fraternity founded in 1986, we continue to uphold our principles of Brotherhood, Scholarship, Community Service, and Cultural Awareness.

Our network serves as a bridge between collegiate members and alumni, fostering professional development, mentorship, and lifelong connections. Through various events and initiatives, we maintain our commitment to excellence and our cultural heritage while supporting the next generation of leaders.
